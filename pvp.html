HOW TO PLAY
MOVEPC: WASD or arrow keys · Phone: left thumb-stick
AIM & FIREPC: mouse to aim, hold left-click to fire · Phone: right thumb-stick to aim + fire
LOADOUTPick your starting gun before you deploy. LASER & CANNON aren't in the loadout — you earn those in-match.
YOUR BAG IS YOUR GUNTap the ⚔ MARKET tab, ape a token — its market cap sets your gun tier. Tokens rug and recover live, so keep an eye on the market.
COVERCrates block bullets. Duck behind them to break line of sight.
FACTIONSYour bullets carry your faction's colour. Winning a round scores a point for your faction in the war.
WINFirst to 5 kills takes the round. The arena then resets for a rematch.
FIGHT A FRIENDShare your invite link, or both join the same ROOM CODE, and battle live.
