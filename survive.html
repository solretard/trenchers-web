HOW TO PLAY
MOVEPC: WASD or arrow keys · Phone: left thumb-stick
AIM & FIREPC: mouse to aim, hold left-click to fire · Phone: right thumb-stick to aim + fire
YOUR BAG IS YOUR GUNTap a token up top to ape it — its market cap sets your gun tier. No bag = a weak pistol.
RUG & RECOVERTokens rug live. Hold through the rug and the bounce re-arms you — bail and you're a jeet.

HEALGrab the glowing ORB to restore morale.
